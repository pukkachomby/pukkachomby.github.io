The Perfect Diet 2

December 10, 2025
Food, Tech

Crafting a perfect diet without AI this time.

There’s bugs in my kale!

Seriously, I kept finding bugs in my fridge and discovered they were coming from my kale bags which, upon inspection, revealed themselves to be kind of more like bug mini-terrariums than actual vessels of food.

Maybe I should be glad that they were grown without any pesticides? Nope, nope, nope. A message to the fine people of Dalat: please put some pesticides back in! I would rather just get the effing cancer.

That aside, I needed to make a new kale-free/bug-free diet, and I decided that the best way to do so was to not use AI assistance this time around. Let’s see how AI’s diet compares to a real human’s.

The Human-Made Diet Plan

The below diet plan contains all the macros, vitamins and minerals that I need without having too much calcium or oxalates, and it’s not too acidic. I also get just the perfect amount of protein, and virtually no bad starches (excluding the chocolate, which is there exclusively for magnesium content cough cough).

Key differences

Gemini’s diet had:

  • Way too much sodium
  • Way too much calcium (had to drop the cheese)
  • Too much chicken (I reduced it by 50%)
  • Too many carrots
  • 1 too many avocados, oranges, and bananas (great for me, because finding a bunch of 6 is rough)
  • Unnecessary olive oil (although I’m kind of not complaining about this)
  • Unnecessary seaweed (also not complaining)
  • Mushrooms and peppers, unless red peppers, turned out to be completely unnecessary
  • No chocolate!

Kale and Nuts

Maybe the worst thing about AI was, admittedly, my input. I completely ruled out all nuts and seeds thinking that all of them were high-oxalate, which is why Gemini had to resort to adding the kale.

In fashioning the diet myself, however, I discovered there there are, in fact, low oxalate seeds (sunflower seeds being among them), and that’s great because those seeds are my #1 source of vitamin E, magnesium, and manganese, making them an essential food for me now.
