## Background

Shower thoughts are deep thoughts that you have while talking a long, hot shower.  After you become a dad, though, your shower thoughts tend to change.

## Dad Shower Thoughts

* How come no one ever talks about Young McDonald?
* Quiet went from being peaceful to terrifying.
* Is my baby crying because she's upset or because I, the backup parent, just walked in the room?
* "Children are so precious." No, *my* kid is precious.  Your kid is a booger-eating mutant.
* Humpty Dumpty is never explicitly referred to as an egg.
* Going shopping alone is a privilege.
* The wheels on the bus are actually completely still for most of the day.
* Getting someone else's poop on me used to be disgusting.
* When the baby is sleeping, I feel like I'm getting away with something.  What?  I don't know.
