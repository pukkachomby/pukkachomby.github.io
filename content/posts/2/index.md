## Recipe

![Roasted sunflower seeds](img/2b.webp)

### Materials
* Raw sunflower seed kernels
* Salt and pepper
* Olive olive or melted butter

### Method
* In an oven-safe casserole dish, layer kernels 1cm high
* Coat with enough fat to cover
* Add salt/pepper to taste
* Air fry @180C for 15 minutes, stir, then cook for another 15 minutes (or until golden to dark brown)
* Cool

### Notes
* Every air fryer is different, so play around with the timing to see what works for you
* It's totally fine to get uneven browning; even kernels that are dark brown still taste really good
* Seeds must cool down before they'll be crunchy
