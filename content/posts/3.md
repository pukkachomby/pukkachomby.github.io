## TL;DR

See it.  5/5 stars.

## Background

Project Hail Mary is about a scientist who goes to space and has to work with a rock alien to save the world.

## Review

I never really felt like Star Trek died.  Star Trek, to me, is like a spirit that inhabits certain shows from time to time.  Some Star Trek episodes (even the older ones) don't have this spirit within them, while other movies like Galaxy Quest do indeed have it.  It's like a ghost that travels from show to show, movie to movie.
