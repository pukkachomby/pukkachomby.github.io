<!--more-->

## Background
I was looking for ways to self-host an image upscale app and happened upon a suggestion to give [this website](https://huggingface.co/spaces) a try.

I was totally blown away by how many tools there are!  Gemini describes the site to me thusly:
* It's like YouTube, but for AI, where "content creators" (ie, AI engineers) showcase their AI "content" (ie, models)
* It has a wide variety of AI tools, ranging from media tools (photo, video, TTS, STT, etc) and text-based tools, like sentiment and document analysis
* Here's the catch for the free tier:
  * You have to wait in line
  * You only get 3-5 minutes of usage per day (processing for about 10-20 images)
  * You can't upload big files for processing
  * If your job is taking too long, the process may get killed

Even with the above restrictions, it still seems pretty awesome and a lot of casual users report that they have a positive experience using it.

## Text
Of course, I want to check out something text-based first.  I found [here](https://huggingface.co/spaces?category=text-summarization&sort=likes) a model which summarizes long texts, and I want to see how it handles an article like [this](https://e.vnexpress.net/news/business/money/i-couldn-t-secure-a-job-for-4-years-despite-graduating-with-master-s-degree-and-8-0-ielts-4993874.html).

Accepting the default parameters, I waited for over 2 minutes before killing the job.  What's the point in waiting when Gemini or Apple Intelligence can do this almost immediately?

I threw this site a soft ball and it already missed!  Hopefully the other models will be stronger.

## Speech to Text
How about switching gears completely and moving to something audio-based?
